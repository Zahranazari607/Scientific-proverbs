Translation: Skepticism drives progress.

Explanation: This highlights the importance of questioning assumptions in science. Doubt leads to deeper inquiry, stronger evidence, and ultimately, advancement.

Translation: Just because something isn't proven yet doesn't mean it's false.

Explanation: This proverb teaches caution in drawing conclusions. Lack of proof doesn't disprove existence—it may simply mean we haven't found it yet.
